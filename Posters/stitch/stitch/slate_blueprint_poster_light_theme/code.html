Abstract

The explosion of scholarly output necessitates a transition from manual screening to automated, LLM-assisted triage protocols.

This research introduces the Slate Blueprint, an orchestration engine designed to handle the nuances of technical literature. We move beyond simple RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to a multi-stage refinement process.

Key Motivations

  • 01

    Scale Mismatch

    Manual triage processes are scaling linearly while publication volume grows exponentially.

  • 02

    Cognitive Friction

    Technical depth in specialized domains often leads to high intra-rater variability.

  • 03

    Hallucination Risk

    Standard LLM prompting lacks the structural grounding required for archival precision.
