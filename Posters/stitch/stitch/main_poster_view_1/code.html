LLM-Assisted Triage

A Multi-Agent Root Cause Analysis Framework for Microservices

Dr. Sarah Chen, Alex Vane SRE Distributed Systems Lab • MIT

book_4 Background

Microservice architectures introduce complex inter-service dependencies, making Root Cause Analysis (RCA) during incidents exceptionally challenging. Traditional methods rely heavily on manual trace and log inspection by Site Reliability Engineers, leading to unacceptably high Mean Time To Resolution (MTTR) during critical outages.

This poster presents a novel multi-agent LLM framework designed to autonomously triage incidents by correlating distributed traces, application logs, and system metrics in real-time.

The Challenge

  • warning Alert Fatigue: Engineers are overwhelmed by cascaded alerts from dependent services.
  • timeline Context Fragmentation: Traces, logs, and metrics exist in siloed observability platforms.
