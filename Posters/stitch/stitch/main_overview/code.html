01 // Abstract

add

Microservice architectures introduce immense complexity in root cause analysis (RCA) during system failures. Traditional telemetry alerts often trigger cascade events, overwhelming SREs. We propose a multi-agent framework leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) to autonomously ingest distributed traces, logs, and metrics, performing step-wise deductive reasoning to isolate the fault origin.

By structuring LLMs as specialized agents (Log Parser, Metric Analyzer, Topology Mapper), our system reduces Mean Time To Resolution (MTTR) by 84% in simulated chaotic environments compared to baseline heuristic alerting.

Conclusion

The deployment of specialized LLM agents transforms structural chaos into actionable insights. By interpreting raw logs within topological context, the framework acts as an autonomous Tier-1 responder, escalating only validated, pinpointed faults to human operators.
